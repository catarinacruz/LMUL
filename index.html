The Experience

We want to sensitize people to the consequences of online hate and make them understand that it has to stop.

Internet allows us to create and share content with everyone. We can easily share our thoughts or ideias online, and receive rapid feedback. But this has opened a window to online hate - as it is so easy to speak your mind, you just do it and walk away. But what happens to the other person? Online hate has severe consequences, and anyone can be a victim. So it's important that we sensitize online users, specially young people, to it's consequences, and if possible, that we put an end to it.

    • The Story

    We are at FEUP, in the academic year of 2016/2017. Four friends attend the course that only the best students at that college can enter - the Master in Industrial Engineering and Management. Veronica, Nashita, Juan and Fernando are a group of great friends, but somehow they see themselves facing the consequences of online hate, and they end up learning that, sometimes, our best friends can also be our worst enemies.

    Veronica had to deal with bullying pretty soon, while she was in high school. Her change from a shy girl to being one of the most popular girls had great advantages, but it all changed when she wanted to meet new people and her so called friends didn’t like her curiosity. They started insulting her and spreading nasty rumours to the entire school, leading Veronica to drink and to cut herself. Although she ends up making peace with one of the bullies, the school continues the attacks, and now they also attack her friend. Only when Veronica realizes she doesn’t want anyone else to suffer the same she did, she finally reports the situation and it all ends, for good.

    When the friends finally graduate from high school, they win a trip to Ibiza and there they meet Juan, who falls for Veronica. Juan is a very nice boy, although kind of depressive and dependent on medication. When he finds out that Veronica is attending the Master’s course in Industrial Engineering and Management at FEUP, he also gains interest for it, and they both apply and enter on the course. They travel together to Porto and spend a great time together, but as soon as classes begin, Veronica starts leaving his company and makes new friends at a local bar she starts working at. Juan jealousy for Veronica’s attention leads him to dangerous paths, as he steals the girl’s Facebook passwords and stars insulting people on her name, leaving the girl devastated. It is only when she catches him spying her conversations that all is revealed, but Veronica is stronger now, and she can forgive Juan for his actions, and understand that is was only too insecure because he liked her so much.

    Later on, Veronica, Juan and Fernando, a third nerdy friend of theirs that is highly competitive, are surprised by the announcement of a Syrian refugee to join them in the course – a girl named Nashita. The girl shows to be nothing like they expected – she’s very social and even cheerful. But they eventually find out she is like that because she learned the hard way to enjoy every bit of life she could, having lost a lot in the war. Nashita also shows to be very competitive, and so she naturally fights with Fernando for the best position at the course - Fernando cannot accept it, since his success is everything for him, and he will not it being taken away from him just because the teachers pity a refugee. His anger leads him to attack Nashita anonymously on Facebook, making her feel like she is the middle of war, once more. But Veronica helps the girl, and they find out who is the attacker. In the end, Fernando and Nashita understand both that their fears was the reason they were attacking each other, and that they did not hate each other. All ends well as the 4 friends grow up together and learn how to become better persons.

    • About the project

    We are a group of 6 University students, put together during a class to create a transmedia storytelling that could cause a social change, in beahaviors or mentalities.

    With this in mind, the team came together with the theme Online Hate and the HateLess Project.

    The main goal is for internet user to understand that on the other side of a computer monitor is always a real person with real feelings and that we need to take responsability for our actions but more importante, before uploading something online we need to think about that we are doing and how is going to afect a person.
